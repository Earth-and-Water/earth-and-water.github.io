Dripstone Pillar

Dripstone Pillar is a decorative Dripstone themed pillar block that generates in Ancient Ruins.

Obtaining

Crafting

Dripstone Pillar can be crafted using regular dripstone blocks in a stonecutter or crafting table:

  • 2 Dripstone Slabs → 2 Dripstone Pillars (Crafting Table)
  • 1 Dripstone Block → 1 Dripstone Pillar (Stonecutter)

It can also be found in various places in the Ancient Ruins structure.

Usage

Dripstone pillars can be placed on any axis.
