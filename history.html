Mod Development History

Concept for E&W

The idea for Earth & Water began in early 2024 when Tricky Trials released. Tricky Trials introduced the Breeze, a mob with similar features to the Blaze. This inspired the creation of the Bore and Brine, which then lead to the rest of the mod being created.

Version 1.0.0

The first major release introduced many blocks, items, and gameplay mechanics that established the core identity of Earth & Water.
