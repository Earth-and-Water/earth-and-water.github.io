Smithing

Equipment

Smithing is a gameplay mechanic originally added in 1.16 for creating Netherite tools, but later was added onto in 1.20, with the smithing templates. Earth & Water adds new smithing templates to further enchance this feature.

Steel Upgrade

The Steel Upgrade is a smithing template that allows players to modify a standard shield into the Spiked Shield. This upgrade adds spikes to the shield, changing both its appearance and functionality.

The Spiked Shield retains all normal blocking abilities of the vanilla shield while adding an offensive property. When struck by an enemy, the spikes can deflect the attack and deal damage back to the attacker.

The smithing recipe combines a shield with a steel ingot and the Steel Upgrade to create the Spiked Shield.

Block Smithing Template

Set of Armor with Block Trim applied to it.

The Block Smithing Template allows you to apply a distinctive block pattern trim to your armor pieces, giving them a bold geometric appearance that emphasizes structure and durability.

The smithing recipe combines a Block Smithing Template with an Iron Chestplate and a Copper Ingot to create the trimmed version of the armor.

Guard Smithing Template

Set of Armor with Guard Trim applied to it.

The Guard Smithing Template applies an elegant protective trim to your armor, featuring reinforced edges and decorative elements that showcase both style and defensive prowess.

The smithing recipe combines a Guard Smithing Template with an Iron Chestplate and a Lapis Lazuli to create the trimmed version of the armor.
