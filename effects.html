Effect List

Effects are gameplay mechanics that provide temporary status changes to players and entities. Earth & Water adds new effects to enhance gameplay.

Stun

The Stun effect slows and weakens the affected entity.

Breath Giving

The Breath Giving effect restores oxygen over the course of a few seconds.
