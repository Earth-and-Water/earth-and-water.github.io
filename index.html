About the Mod

Earth & Water is a mod that expands on elemental mobs. Minecraft has the Blaze and Breeze, which fills in half the elemental mobs. E&W adds the other two elemental mobs, the Bore and Brine! These two mobs come with their own blocks and structures that fit them respectively.

The Bore dwells in Ancient Ruins, shooting Earth Charges and burrowing underground when threatened. The Brine lurks in Conduit Monuments, dashing through water and launching explosive Water Charges. The mod introduces new weapons like the dashing Battle Axe, the stunning Whip, and the damage-reflecting Spiked Shield. Some of these are crafted with Steel, a sort of upgraded iron.

Featured Article

Reinforced Spawners are special spawner blocks that create infinite waves of mobs and cannot be broken by conventional means. Found in Ancient Ruins and Conduit Monuments, these spawners continuously generate Bores and Brines respectively, making them challenging but rewarding structures to explore.

E&W also adds new structures containing these spawners, plus expanded block sets including Dripstone Bricks, Dark Dripstone variants, and Prismarine Tiles that complement the elemental theme of the mod.
